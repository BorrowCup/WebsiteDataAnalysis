Cups Usage Pattern

The following Arc diagram shows the BorrowCups' usage pattern from week to week using the data collected post 22nd Jan 2020. The donut charts (circles) represent weeks and the arcs represent the weeks a set of cups were used and their proportions.

For example, the arc between Week 4 and 5 indicates the number of cups used in Week 4 and then in Week 5. Similarly, the arc between Week 5 and 9 indicates the number of cups used in Week 5 that were not used again until Week 9. The width of the arc is proportionate to the number of cups used. The donut charts representing weeks shows the distribution of the cups used once in a week in Turquoise, cups with 2 or more uses in a week in Skyblue, cups used solely in a week (out of all weeks) in Greenyellow colour.
