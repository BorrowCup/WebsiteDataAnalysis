Reuse Proportions

The following venn diagrams shows the proportions of cups' uses per week. Each circle represents a category: Once, Twice or Thrice (per week). It is evident from the diagrams below that most of the cups have only been used Once a week, some 10 to 40 percent were used either Once or Twice, and only a minuscule amount of cups were used three times in some weeks. The numbers on the diagrams denote the amount of cups inside each disjoint group. However, the precentages that appear on mouse hover denote the percentage of cups (out of total cups) inside the highlighted boundary.

Due to missing records for sales, the sales venn diagram doesn't represent the true or a highly accurate picture, and should be used with caution. To help you with this, the returns venn diagram has also been provided. The caveat here is that the two returns in a week doesn't necessarily mean two sales in that week.
